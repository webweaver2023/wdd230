Content

Home page

Welcome
The ‘Home’ page will provide a landing spot to welcome visitors. Included on the ‘Home’ page will be a welcome message with a brief explanation/mission statement of what the goal of Clear the Clutter is as an organization including the desire to work with clients to solve organizational problems throughout the home.

Our goal at Clear the Clutter is to help people resolve organizational issues—large or small. We work with our clients to find the best use for different areas of the home and outline the steps needed to get there. Let us work for you to make the most of your space and discover a new sense of clarity, beauty and ease as we aspire to Clear the Clutter in your home.

Services
Visitors will be looking for the services that are offered. One prompt link will be displayed for easily discovering the exact services that are offered and the costs associated with services.

Clear the Clutter introduces long-lasting solutions to any area of your home. Whether we unpack and set up your whole home after a move, or just set systems in place for specific rooms in your home, you can relax knowing you’re in great hands. Check out our services here.

Testimonials
When people are looking at a product or a service, they want to know they are getting quality. Testimonials give assurances that clients will receive professional treatment. Including a testimonial on the ‘Home’ page along with a link to additional testimonials will be another instrument to draw people in.

“Absolutely loved what Clear the Clutter did for our home. Why will people come to this site?
The 'Home' page will set the precendent for showing quality images. Because this is an organizational services website, it is imperative that all information is well organized, easy to navigate and visually appealing. Images will be vital in drawing potential clients to the website with a need to show highly organized spaces that inspire viewers to see the potential for their own homes getting organized.

Services

Our Services
Clear the Clutter is a premier home organization and relocation company. We excel at creating beautiful, functional, and organized spaces in our client's homes, working one-on-one with you prior to the job and on the day of the job. We love to guide clients on creating sustainable practices to stay organized even after our services end.

The Process
Initial Consultaion-We start with every client by assessing organizational needs. Potential clients will upload photos of their space, and inspiration for their project. In the initial consult, we discuss the space, budget, and what you're looking for in a partner to achieve organizational goals.

Proposal-Once we have a good idea of your needs and wishes, we'll send you a proposal for the space. The proposal will detail the hours and supplies needed to complete your project to your satisfaction. Once a contract is agreed upon, a date is booked for your in-home organization project.

Organizing-On the date scheduled, our team will arrive with everything in hand to de-clutter, sort, label and place where you will be able to sustain your organization needs moving forward.

Enjoy Your Space-We remove all trash and donate any items you no longer need; leaving you with a space that you can enjoy for years to come.
