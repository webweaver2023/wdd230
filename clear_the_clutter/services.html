Clear the Clutter

Clear the Clutter is a premier home organization and relocation company. We excel at creating beautiful, functional, and organized spaces in our client's homes, working one-on-one with you prior to the job and on the day of the job. We love to guide clients on creating sustainable practices to stay organized even after our services end.

The process...

INITIAL CONSULTATION

We start with every client by assessing organizational needs. Potential clients will upload photos of their space, and inspiration for their project. In the initial consult, we discuss the space, budget, and what you're looking for in a partner to achieve organizational goals.

PROPOSAL

Once we have a good idea of your needs and wishes, we'll send you a proposal for the space. The proposal will detail the hours and supplies needed to complete your project to your satisfaction. Once a contract is agreed upon, a date is booked for your in-home organization project.

ORGANIZING

On the date scheduled, our team will arrive with everything in hand to de-clutter, sort, label and place where you will be able to sustain your organization needs moving forward.

ENJOY YOUR SPACE

We remove all trash and donate any items you no longer need; leaving you with a space that you can enjoy for years to come.
